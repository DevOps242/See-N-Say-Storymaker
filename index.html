How to build a See-N-Say Story!

1. Click 'Next' to go through the word choices in the Subject column.

2. When you reach the word you wish to use press 'Save'.

3. Click 'Next' again to begin going through the Verb column, and press 'Save' to select the word to use in your story.

4. Repeat Steps 1 and 2 for the Adjective, Thing and Place column.

5. Once you've saved a word from each column, click 'Play' to hear your story.

6. To create another story, click 'Reset', then 'Save'.
